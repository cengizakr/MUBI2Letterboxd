Good to know

Do I need to log in here?
No. This site never touches your Letterboxd account — you upload the CSV yourself.
Does it work with private watchlists?
No — your MUBI profile must be public.
Is my data stored?
Never. The CSV is generated in memory and discarded after download.

Where it lands on Letterboxd

Letterboxd supports importing the same CSV into three different places, each with its own entry point. Sign in to Letterboxd first, then follow the steps for the destination you picked. Full reference: letterboxd.com/about/importing-data.

Watchlist

Letterboxd watchlist

Mirror your MUBI watchlist as upcoming films you plan to watch.

  1. Sign in to Letterboxd, then open letterboxd.com/<your-username>/watchlist/.
  2. Use the watchlist's Import action (only visible to you as the owner).
  3. Upload the downloaded CSV and confirm.

Letterboxd imports the films straight into your watchlist — no list workaround needed.

Watched

Watched films

Mark every MUBI title as already seen in your Letterboxd profile.

  1. Open letterboxd.com/import.
  2. Upload the downloaded CSV.
  3. Review the matches on the preview screen and confirm.

All imported films are marked as watched and affect your diary and stats — there is no undo.

List

New custom list

Turn your MUBI watchlist into a standalone Letterboxd list you can share or keep private.

  1. Create a list at letterboxd.com/list/new.
  2. On the list's edit page, use Import a list from a CSV file and upload the CSV.
  3. Name the list and save.

Manage your lists at letterboxd.com/<your-username>/lists/.
