Watchlist transfer

Move your MUBI watchlist to Letterboxd

Paste your MUBI profile link. This creates a small file that Letterboxd can import into your watchlist.

Works with links like https://mubi.com/en/users/10335289, watchlist links, or just the profile number.

1

Find your MUBI profile

Open MUBI in your browser, go to your profile page, and copy the address from the top bar.

2

Download the file

Paste the profile link above. Your browser will download a file named for Letterboxd.

3

Import on Letterboxd

Open Letterboxd import, choose the downloaded file, and import it as your watchlist.

What is the downloaded file?

It is a simple spreadsheet-style file with movie titles and years. You do not need to open or edit it; Letterboxd reads it during import.
