Good to know

Do I need to log in?
No. The tool only reads your public watchlist.
Does it work with private watchlists?
No — your MUBI profile must be public.
Is my data stored?
Never. The CSV is generated in memory and discarded after download.
What do I do with the file?
Upload it at letterboxd.com/import.

What to do next

  1. i

    Download the CSV. Paste your MUBI link above and click Download CSV.

  2. ii

    Open Letterboxd import. Go to letterboxd.com/import.

  3. iii

    Upload the file. Choose the downloaded CSV and import the rows as watchlist items.
